My name is Yaotang(Johnny), I was born in Guangzhou China and moved to the US when I was a kid. During my childhood I pursued out-of-school activities like basketball, video-games, and selling candy. After graduating from Cupertino High School I went to University of California Riverside to obtain a degree in Business with a specialization in marketing.

Being an immigrant and a first-generation college student has cultivated an ambitious and competitive mentality in my pursuit of a degree and job opportunities. Throughout my academic career I have been able to demonstrate my strong work ethic and dedication to overcoming obstacles. Being an immigrant and tackling issues that comes with being an immigrant has instilled in me a drive to push forward regardless of hardships. Needing to learn english and dealing with cultural challenges are just some examples of barriers I overcame that ignited a need to succeed, not only with my own personal but academic and societal experiences. It is through my numerous challenges that have given me the motivation to continuoslly strive to achieve goals.

My diverse background allows me to incorporate a broader range of skills to bring to any academic or professional settings. Learning english through text was important to me, but practicing it verbally was just as crucial. Learning english has allowed me to deepen my communication skills which I can bring to collabrative projects and overall allows me to work efficiently. Always having to translate for my parents and catching up to my peers when I moved from China has taught me how to work under preassure. I am excited to be able to incorporate my skills into my work and communication in order to make a meaningful impact on the business marketing world.
